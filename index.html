WHO WE ARE


We are a group of driven, innovative, and creative individuals. As a STEM business fraternity, we foster cross-discipline collaboration and community.

Welcome to the Alpha Chapter of Iota Omega Epsilon, Michigan’s premier STEM-Business co-ed professional fraternity. As President, I am honored to extend a warm welcome to you on behalf of our fraternity!

During my first year at Michigan, I wanted to join a community that seeked to combine their engineering knowledge and business skills to create positive change both locally and globally. I was able to find this in Iota, and I can confidently say that joining has been the most rewarding and impactful part of my college experience. Not only have I been introduced to lifelong friends and connections who serve as a constant source of inspiration, I’ve also been provided the guidance and resources to become the best version of myself.

As Michigan’s first STEM-Business fraternity, Iota Omega Epsilon offers brothers a community of like-minded and driven individuals, along with the tools they need to succeed in the ever-evolving world of STEM and business. From project teams and study groups to professional development workshops, we cultivate a culture of growth where brothers are pushed to become the best version of themselves and supported in their pursuit of their passions within STEM and Business.

Thank you for taking the time to visit our website. I encourage you to continue exploring to learn more about who we are and what we stand for. If you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to reach out to me at eten@umich.edu. We look forward to meeting you!

Best,
Ethan George
